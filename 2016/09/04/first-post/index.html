Ok here we are. This is the first post in my new blog. I hope this space to become a share of thoughts about Software Engineering in practice. I will be posting about things I am mostly interested in, such as Web development, Information Security, System Programming or anything relative to modern Software Development Process.

This site will also support comments so feel free to write your oppinion as long as its not spam or trolling or hate.

From time to time I plan to write tutorials that I find that they can be usefull. Also I will be writing about my personal projects that I care to explain more in detail.

I hope that my share of knowledge will help future developers in tackling real life issues.

You can find more things about me at https://theodespoudis.com.
